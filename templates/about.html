✨Our Story: The Birth of Panduta 🚀

Panduta was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to make life easier, one clean garment at a time. 🧺✨

A Wrinkle in Time ⏳

It all started with Susie, a passionate fullstack engineering student at ALX Africa. 👩‍💻 Juggling her intensive studies, personal life, and household chores, Susie found herself constantly struggling with the never-ending pile of laundry. 😓 One hectic day, as she rushed to pick up her dry cleaning before closing time, she thought, "There has to be a better way." 💡

Weaving a Solution 🕸️

Inspired by her coursework at ALX Africa, Susie envisioned a platform that could connect people with the best laundry and dry cleaning services in their area. 🌟

Launch Day

We'll officially launch Panduta in 2024 Q4, marking the beginning of a new era in laundry services.
